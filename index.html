Quick Reference

tPA Window

IV tPA: Within 4.5 hours of symptom onset

Thrombectomy: Up to 24 hours in select patients (DAWN/DEFUSE-3 criteria)

tPA Dosing

Dose: 0.9 mg/kg (max 90 mg)

Administration: 10% bolus over 1 min, 90% infusion over 60 min

BP Targets

Pre-tPA: < 185/110 mmHg

Post-tPA (24h): < 180/105 mmHg

No tPA: Permissive HTN unless > 220/120

tPA Contraindications

  • Recent surgery/trauma (14 days)
  • Active bleeding or bleeding diathesis
  • Platelets < 100,000
  • INR > 1.7 or PT > 15s
  • Recent stroke (3 months)
  • Prior ICH
