Quick Reference

tPA Window

IV tPA: Within 4.5 hours of onset

Thrombectomy: Up to 24h (DAWN/DEFUSE-3)

tPA Dosing

Dose: 0.9 mg/kg (max 90 mg)

Admin: 10% bolus, 90% over 60 min

BP Targets

Pre-tPA: <185/110 mmHg

Post-tPA: <180/105 mmHg

Contraindications

  • Recent surgery/trauma (14d)
  • Platelets <100,000
  • INR >1.7
  • Prior ICH
